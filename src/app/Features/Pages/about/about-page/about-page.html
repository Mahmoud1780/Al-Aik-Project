Who We Are

Al Aik Landscaping & pools services has over 29 years in UAE. We are specialized in offering complete solution in Landscape and swimming pools services from design, build to maintaining your landscape and pools.

We ensure that each project is carried out to the highest professional standard with attention given to every detail, our dedicated and motivated team of professionals never stops creating new concepts and to introducing new trends, guides through each step of the project. Our experience and skill ensure customers satisfaction at all time.

Al Aik has experience delivering wide range of projects over the years from Residential & Commercial Building to Hotels, Parks, Resorts, Schools, Governments institutions, Palaces and Farms.

Our Story

Al Aik was established in UAE in the year 1994 as Irrigation specialist and extended its services as Landscaping & swimming Pool Specialist in the year 2014. With years of experience we have successfully delivered residential and commercial projects in UAE for our customers with satisfaction and to highest quality standard.
